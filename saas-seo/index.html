The AI Search Advantage

What AI search rewards differently.

80% of what Google rewards is what AI rewards. But the 20% that's different matters.

Entity recognition

LLMs need to recognize your brand as an entity before they can recommend it. Most SaaS brands are invisible to LLMs because they've never been entity-optimized.

Information uniqueness

LLMs prioritize content with original research, unique data points, and proprietary frameworks. If your content says what 50 other articles say, the LLM has no reason to cite you.

Structured extractability

LLMs pull information differently than Google indexes it. Clear definitions, direct answers, and attributable claims get cited more. Content structured for extraction wins.

The visibility lag: LLMs train on content from 6-18 months ago. Authority you build today shows up as AI citations 6-18 months from now. Every month of inaction is a month competitors train future models to recommend them instead.
