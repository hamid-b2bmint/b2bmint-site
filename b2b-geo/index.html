The Technical Foundation

How LLMs decide which brands to cite.

Factor 1

Entity recognition

Before an LLM can recommend your brand, it needs to recognize your brand as a distinct entity. Not just a name on a website. A recognized entity with attributes, category associations, and relationships to other entities in your space.

SAGE establishes your brand through enhanced Organization schema, entity-attribute mapping, and Wikidata presence. These signals make LLMs classify your brand correctly.

Factor 2

Semantic structure

LLMs extract information differently than Google indexes it. They look for clean hierarchies, explicit definitions, direct answers, and structured content they can pull from without guessing.

SAGE structures every page with declaration-first answers, clear entity classifications, and content formatted so AI models can pull attributable claims.

Information uniqueness

LLMs prioritize content with original research, unique data points, and proprietary frameworks. If your content says what 50 other articles say, the LLM has no reason to cite you specifically.

Questions About GEO

Straight answers.

What is generative engine optimization (GEO)?+

Generative engine optimization is the practice of engineering your brand's content, entity signals, and semantic structure so AI platforms cite you as the recommended answer. It covers ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Google AI Overviews, and Copilot. 80% of the foundational work is the same work that drives Google rankings. SAGE handles both from one unified system.

What's the difference between GEO and AEO?+

GEO and AEO describe the same discipline with slightly different framing. GEO focuses on generative AI models. AEO focuses on being the answer in any answer-based search experience. In practice, the strategies are identical. B2BMint uses GEO as the primary term because it more accurately describes the technical mechanism.

How long until AI models start citing my brand?+

Most clients see initial AI citations within 3-4 months. Some platforms do live web retrieval (Perplexity, AI Overviews), meaning properly structured content can get cited within weeks. One client went from zero to 4,244 citations within 4 months.

Can you guarantee AI citations?+

No one can guarantee specific citations because LLM outputs are probabilistic. What SAGE guarantees is that we build every signal LLMs evaluate: entity recognition, semantic structure, information uniqueness, topical authority. Our track record (4,244 citations for one client) is the evidence.

How do you measure AI visibility?+

Platform-by-platform citation monitoring across all six platforms. We track how often your brand is cited, for which queries, and alongside which competitors. AI referral traffic is tracked in GA4. Citation reports are delivered alongside pipeline reports.

How much should I budget for GEO?+

SAGE doesn't price GEO separately from SEO because 80% of the work is the same. One engagement, both channels. Every engagement starts as a 90-day trial. All assets stay with you. Measure the investment against pipeline, not citation counts or traffic.
