The Situation

$50K spent. 73 clicks a day. Zero demos.

This client came to us 9 months after raising their Series A. They’d spent $50K with a respected SaaS SEO agency that published 40+ blog posts. Traffic reports showed green arrows. The CFO asked one question: how many demos came from this? Answer: zero.

The previous agency had optimized for MOFU keyword volume while their buyers were searching for comparison pages, alternatives, and pricing. On AI platforms, the brand was completely invisible. ChatGPT recommended competitors with half the product.

The company was three quarters from needing a Series B story. They needed organic search to become a meaningful pipeline channel, not a traffic line item.
