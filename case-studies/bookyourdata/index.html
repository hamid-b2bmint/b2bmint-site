The Situation

Bootstrapped. Outgunned. Invisible.

BookYourData is a bootstrapped B2B data provider competing in a market dominated by heavily-funded players like ZoomInfo and Apollo. No venture funding. No massive ad budget. The company needed organic search to become its primary growth channel.

When B2BMint started, the company was generating around $1M in annual revenue. Organic visibility was limited. The most valuable commercial keywords were controlled by enterprise incumbents with 10x the budget and 100x the domain authority.

The SAGE Engine at Work

What B2BMint built over 3+ years.
