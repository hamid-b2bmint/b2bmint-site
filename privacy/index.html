1. Who we are

B2BMint is operated by Build Goat LLC, a Delaware-registered company, with a supporting team at Build Vista Pvt. Ltd. (Pakistan). We provide SaaS SEO and AI visibility services for B2B SaaS companies.

This policy applies to b2bmint.com and any services we offer under the B2BMint name. If you’re reading this, you either visited our site, booked a call, subscribed to our newsletter, or are a client. All of those are covered below.

2. What we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Contact details — name, email, company, and job title when you book a call, subscribe to our newsletter, or message us.
  • Client data — if you become a client, we collect the information needed to deliver the engagement: company details, access to analytics tools (Google Search Console, GA4, etc.), CMS access if agreed, and the content of our working sessions.
  • Payment details — processed by our payment processor. We don’t store full card numbers.

Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on page, referrer, approximate location (from IP), device type, browser.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see section 5.

3. How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule calls.
  • Deliver the services you’ve hired us for.
  • Send the newsletter if you’ve subscribed.
  • Understand which pages and content resonate so we can improve them.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, accounting, etc.).

We don’t use your data for profiling, scoring, or automated decision-making that has a significant effect on you.

4. Who we share it with

We only share data with service providers who help us run the business. We don’t sell your data.

  • Scheduling — Calendly (when you book a call).
  • Email — the provider that sends our newsletter and client communications.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics / Microsoft Clarity to understand site usage.
  • Payments — our payment processor, for client invoicing.
  • Hosting & infrastructure — the services that host this website and our client tooling.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or if necessary to protect our rights or the safety of others.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for two purposes: making the site work (functional) and measuring how people use it (analytics). You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer. Functional cookies may break some features if disabled; analytics cookies have no effect on functionality.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct data that’s inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where consent is the basis of processing.
  • Complain to a data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hamid@b2bmint.com. We’ll respond within 30 days.

7. How long we keep data

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected:

  • Newsletter subscribers — until you unsubscribe.
  • Leads who didn’t become clients — up to 2 years, then deleted.
  • Clients — duration of the engagement plus 7 years for tax/accounting records, unless legally required longer.
  • Analytics data — typically 14-26 months depending on the tool.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and vetted third-party providers. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access.

9. International transfers

Our team operates from the US (Delaware) and Pakistan. Some of our service providers are based in the US, EU, and elsewhere. When data moves across borders, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve or as laws change. The “last updated” date at the top reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be communicated to active clients and newsletter subscribers.
